Soft Body Simulation

Soft body simulations are often difficult to implement due to their complex mechanical changes. Matyka and Ollila, n.d. proposed a method for simulating soft bodies by combining particle systems and ideal gas approximation in their article 'Pressure Model of Soft Body Simulation', which simplified the simulation of complex problems. This project applied this method in this project to simulate the collision process between a soft sphere and the ground after a free fall.
